Biography

I am currently a 3rd-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young since Fall 2019. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree majored in Electronics Science and Technology (Talented Student Program) from South China University of Technology. My research interests lie primarily in GPU accelerated Placement and Routing (PnR) in VLSI Physical Design. I am also interested in distributed deep learning systems.
