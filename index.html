Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from CUHK in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young, and my B.Eng. degree from South China University of Technology in 2019. My earlier research focused on placement and routing (PnR) in VLSI physical design. Currently, I am primarily working on GPU-accelerated VLSI EDA. I am also interested in LLM algorithm-system co-optimization.
