Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow advised by Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2023 and my B.Eng. degree from South China University of Technology in 2019. My research interests lie primarily in Placement and Routing (PnR) in VLSI Physical Design and distributed machine learning systems.
